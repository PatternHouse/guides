Pattern 1

The Pattern
1 4 7 10 13 16 19 . . .

Logic
Clearly the above pattern is increasing by 3. This also called an Arithmetic Progression (AP) .
The initial integer is 1, the next one is 1 + 3 = 4, and the next one 1 + 2 * 3 = 6 and so on. Every next term is given by (initial term) + (index of the term - 1) * (common difference). This is also called the general term of an AP. The common difference can also be negative.
